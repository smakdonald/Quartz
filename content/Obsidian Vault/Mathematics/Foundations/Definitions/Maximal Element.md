Let $P$ be a non-empty [[Family of Sets|family]] of [[Set|sets]]. Let $M\in P$[^2]. The set $M$ is a *maximal* [[Element|element]] of $P$ if there is no $Q\in P$ such that $M\subsetneq Q$[^1].
