Given square matrices $A_1 \in \Mat_{n_1 \times n_1}(F), \dots, A_m \in \Mat_{n_m \times n_m}(F)$, we define 
$A_1 \oplus \cdots \oplus A_m$ to be the *block diagonal* matrix
$$
\begin{bmatrix} 
A_1 & 0 & \cdots & 0 \\
0 & A_2 &  \cdots & 0 \\
\vdots & & \ddots & \vdots \\
0 & \cdots & 0 & A_m \\
\end{bmatrix}
$$
which belongs to $\Mat_{n \times n}(F)$ for $n = \sum_i n_i$.
