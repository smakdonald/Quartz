Let $R$ be a [[Ring|ring]] (with $1$). A  *left $R$-module* is an [[Abelian Group|abelian]] [[Group|group]] $(M,+)$ together with a pairing $R \times M \to M$, written $(r,m) \mapsto rm$, such that for all $r,s \in R$ and $m,n \in M$
1. $(r + s)m = rm + sm$,
2. $(rs)m = r(sm)$,
3. $r(m + n) = rm + rn$, and 
4. $1m = m$.
