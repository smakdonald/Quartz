A *ring* is a set $R$ equipped with two [[Binary Operation|binary operations]], $+$ and $\cdot$, satisfying:
1. $(R,+)$ is an [[Definitions#Abelian Group|abelian]] [[Group|group]] with identity element denoted $0$,
2. $\cdot$ is associative (making $(R,\cdot)$ a semigroup)
3. $a \cdot (b + c) = a \cdot b + a \cdot c$ and $(a + b) \cdot c = a \cdot c + b \cdot c$ hold for all $a,b,c \in R$.
