#### $\cor$ – The Prime Divisor Property
For $b, c \in\Z$[^1], if $p$ is a [[Prime|prime]] with $p | bc$[^2], then $p | b$ or $p | c$.

##### *Proof.*
Suppose p | bc. If p | b we are done. If p  b, then \gcd( p, b) = 1, so by  
Euclid’s Lemma (Lemma 3.2.23), p | c.

[^1]: Notation: [[Integers]]
[^2]: Notation: [[Divides]]
