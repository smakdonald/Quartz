#### $\cor$ – Orders in $\Z/(n)$ and $\varphi(n)$
If $[a] \in\Z/(n)^{\times}$[^1], then $|[a]|\, \big|\, \varphi(n)$[^2].

##### *Proof.*
This gem comes directly from the proof of Euler’s Theorem.
