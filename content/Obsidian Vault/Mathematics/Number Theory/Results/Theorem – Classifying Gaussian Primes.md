The primes of $\Z[i]$ are, up to associates, precisely:
- The prime $(1 + i)$.
- The rational primes p that are congruent to $3 \mod 4$.
- The primes $a + bi$ and $a - bi$, where $a^{2} + b^{2} = p \equiv 1 \mod 4$ and $p$ is a rational prime.
