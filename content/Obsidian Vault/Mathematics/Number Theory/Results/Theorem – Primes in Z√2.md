#### $\thm$ – Primes in Z√2
The primes of $\Z[\sqrt{2}]$ are, up to associates, precisely:
- The prime $\sqrt{2}$.
- The rational primes $p$ that are congruent to $3$ or $5 \mod 8$.
- The primes $a + b \sqrt{2}$ and $a - b \sqrt{2}$, where $a^{2} - 2b^{2} = \pm p \equiv \pm1 \mod 8$.
