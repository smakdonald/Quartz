For all $u, v \in\N$[^1] with $u > v$, the triple $(u^{2} - v^{2}, 2uv, u^{2} + v^{2})$ is a [[Pythagorean Triple]]. Further, every Pythagorean triple $(a, b, c) \in\N^{3}$ is a multiple of one of this form.
